Flash firmware, Wi-Fi, and your own photos in one flow.

Select a board, add photos, set Wi-Fi and Pomodoro defaults, then flash over USB.

On-device Pomodoro & modes

After flashing, use the companion button to switch views and control the timer. The default Pomodoro length below is loaded on boot; you can still change it on the device.

  • Short press — Cycle: clock → Pomodoro → weather → clock.
  • Double press — Add 5 minutes to the Pomodoro length (opens Pomodoro view).
  • Triple press — Subtract 5 minutes from the Pomodoro length (opens Pomodoro view).
  • Long press — Start or pause the Pomodoro countdown (opens Pomodoro view).

Weather uses Open-Meteo at the coordinates for your selected timezone. The device must be on Wi-Fi for live weather.
