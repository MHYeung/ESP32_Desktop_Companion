ESP32 Desktop Companion

Flash firmware, Wi-Fi, photos, and Pomodoro defaults in one flow.

Pick your board, enter Wi‑Fi credentials, tune Pomodoro if you like, add photos, then flash over USB. After first boot on Wi‑Fi, the device sets the clock and weather location from your network IP—no manual timezone.

Device & Wi‑Fi

Clock & weather region. On Wi‑Fi, the firmware queries your public IP (same request as weather) and applies the current UTC offset for local time, including seasonal DST when the geolocation API reports an updated offset. VPNs and captive portals move your apparent location.

Pomodoro (defaults on boot)

On-device controls

Use the companion button to switch views and run the timer. Double/triple press adjusts only the focus session (+/− 5 minutes).

  • Short press — Cycle: clock → Pomodoro → weather → clock.
  • Double press — Add 5 minutes to the focus session (opens Pomodoro).
  • Triple press — Subtract 5 minutes from the focus session (opens Pomodoro).
  • Long press — Pause or resume the current Pomodoro phase.

Weather uses Open-Meteo with coordinates from ipapi.co. The device must be on Wi‑Fi for live weather and IP-based local time.
